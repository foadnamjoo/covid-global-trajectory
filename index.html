This interactive visualization combines a choropleth world map with a time‑series line chart to reveal how COVID‑19 spread across countries and continents.

Click on a country on the map to compare its trend in the line chart.

Hover over the map to see each country's peak cases per million.

Move your mouse over the line chart to inspect exact values over time.

Clear the selection to empty the chart. Use "Select all" or pick countries to see trends.
